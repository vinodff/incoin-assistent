How It Works

Three simple steps to get your workflow up and running in minutes.

01

Create & Manage Tasks

Sign up and start creating tasks immediately. Organise them by type, priority, or status. The platform adapts to how you work.

02

Use Credits to Process

Each action in the platform consumes credits. Purchase the plan that fits your volume. Credits are flexible and never expire mid-cycle.
