Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2025  |  Please review this policy before making a purchase.

1. Overview

Incoin Assistant provides a credit-based digital utility service. Credits are digital goods that are delivered instantly upon successful payment. Please read this Refund Policy carefully before making any purchase on the platform.

By completing a payment on Incoin Assistant, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2. Nature of Credits

  • Credits are digital units used exclusively to access features within the Incoin Assistant platform.
  • Credits have no monetary value and cannot be converted to cash, bank transfers, or any other asset.
  • Credits are delivered digitally and instantly upon successful payment completion.
  • Due to the immediate digital delivery nature, credits are generally non-refundable once issued to your account.

3. No Refund After Credit Usage

Once credits have been credited to your account and used (partially or fully) to process tasks or access platform features, no refund will be issued under any circumstances. This applies to:

  • Credits used to process any task on the platform.
  • Credits partially consumed before a refund request.
  • Credits expired or removed due to a violation of the Terms and Conditions.
  • Dissatisfaction with platform results or task outcomes.

4. Refund Eligibility — Failed Transactions

A refund will only be considered in the following specific scenario:

  • Your payment was successfully deducted from your account, and
  • The corresponding credits were not delivered to your Incoin Assistant account within 24 hours.

In this case, you are entitled to a full refund or credit allocation as per our verification process.

5. How to Request a Refund

To raise a refund request for a failed transaction, please follow these steps:

  • Email us at vcontenthelper@gmail.com within 7 days of the transaction.
  • Include your registered email address and the transaction details (amount, date, and Razorpay transaction ID).
  • Our support team will review your request within 3–5 business days.
  • If the failure is confirmed, a refund will be processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days, subject to your bank's processing timelines.

6. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Credits were successfully delivered but not used as expected.
  • Refund request is made after 7 days of the transaction date.
  • Account was suspended due to a violation of our Terms and Conditions.
  • User claims platform did not meet subjective expectations.
  • Duplicate purchases made due to user error.
  • Credits were used to access any platform feature, even partially.

7. Razorpay Payment Gateway

All payments are processed through Razorpay, a secure and RBI-compliant payment gateway. Incoin Assistant does not have direct access to your payment credentials. Refunds, where applicable, are initiated through Razorpay and subject to the payment gateway's processing timelines.

For payment-related disputes, you may also contact Razorpay's support at razorpay.com/support.

8. Chargebacks

We strongly encourage users to contact us before initiating a chargeback with their bank. Unauthorised chargebacks for credits that were successfully delivered will be disputed, and accounts involved in fraudulent chargebacks will be permanently suspended.

9. Policy Updates

Incoin Assistant reserves the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with the updated date. Continued use of the platform signifies acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

For any refund-related queries, please contact us at: vcontenthelper@gmail.com

We aim to respond to all support requests within 24–48 business hours.
